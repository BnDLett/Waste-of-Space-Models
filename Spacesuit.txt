About it
The spacesuit is designed to be used in space, allowing the ability to move up and down, along with anchoring yourself to stop all motion.

Controlling it
The green button moves you up.
The red button moves you down.
The yellow button anchors you.
You can take off the suit by clicking your torso (apparel part)

Before use, ensure you set the antenna id's. The antennas' are on the bottom left and right.
